Originele recensie (English):
I think I've never read an almost-500-pages-book that fast.
There were some parts in this book where I couldn't get through as easy, but the rest I flew through!
I loved Nicholas so much! He was really sweet and protective.
Etta's mom, I didn't like so much. How could she not tell Etta anything about what she was going to get through! But the way Alexandra Bracken had created the characters was so so good!

I highly recommend this book!
