Laslo Strange is an orphan, found abandoned when he was a baby.
He grows up to be a librarian, with an obsession for the Lost City, known only as Weep.

When he gets the chance to go to this city, his whole life changes.

Laini Taylor weaves an incredible lyrical tale about hopes, dreams, wishes and fairy tales.
This is not a book you devour in a few hours. You take your time to enjoy the beautiful writing, characters, world building and plot line.
There are new things to discover around every corner and the way everything comes together at the end has you screaming for the next book.
