Originele recensie (English):
I've never read a 400+ book that fast!!
This book was so so good!!!
I loved some chapters they would focus on Cinder en the other chapter focusses on Scarlet and Wolf. I immediately noticed the Red Riding Hood inspired scene when it happened and I thought that Marissa Meyer had blend it into the story very well!

There was a character in this book that I didn't know was going to be in Scarlet and it's already my favorite character!
In my 'The Queen's Army' review I wondered how Ran and Wolf's realtionship would develop. It wasn't a very surprising development, but their relationship did kinda suprise my towards the middle/end.
And then there's Iko. The sweet, loving Iko warmed my heart once again! She has always been trying so hard to be a human and that's so adorable!

Was Scarlet better than Cinder? First I thought so. Now I'm not sure. I think they are equally as good!
I've read three Lunar stories so far and I don't regret having already bought the entire series!
