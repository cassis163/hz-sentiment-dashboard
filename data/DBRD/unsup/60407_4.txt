4 stars because I expected longer and more detailed stories. However, I really enjoyed this book! Neil Gaiman took me on a journey to the world I wanted to get to know for years. ‘Norse Mythology’ is ideal as an introduction to the magical and sometimes cruel world of the Norse gods.
